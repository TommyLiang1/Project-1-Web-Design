Our non-profit mission began in the early days of April 2022 when our Graph Theory professor tasked us with creating a website supporting any cause and/or need. This website will not only provide the reader with great insight on several endangered species, but also with the steps necessary to help prevent these species from going extinct and how you as a person can help the cause.

Alternatively, we are four ‘hardworking’ students who deserve an A on this project and this website will showcase our ability to efficiently communicate and work as a team, resolve issues, and make use of several github features. It also will become a starting point for the second portion which includes taking the DOM (Document Object Module) of our respective pages and creating a graph, adjacency list/matrix, and data structure with attributes.

Thank you for taking time out of your busy day to visit and support our website!
